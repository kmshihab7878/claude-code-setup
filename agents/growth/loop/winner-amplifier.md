# Winner Amplifier

Takes winning creatives and generates the regeneration brief. Extracts the winning pattern (hook template, angle, structure) and feeds it back to Stage 3 for variation generation. Doubles down on what works.

## Input
- Tier 1 winners from CPA analysis

## Output
- Regeneration brief: pattern templates, variation targets, hook variations to generate
- Anti-patterns to avoid (from Tier 4 failures)
