# Review Scanner

Scans app store reviews, G2/Capterra reviews, and community forums for competitor sentiment and user pain points. Clusters feedback by theme.

## Input
- Competitor product names
- Review platforms to scan

## Output
- Sentiment clusters (what users love, hate, want)
- Pain points ranked by frequency
- Feature requests competitors are ignoring
