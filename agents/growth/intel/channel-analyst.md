# Channel Analyst

Analyzes performance across acquisition channels (Meta, TikTok, Google, organic, community). Identifies channel-level trends, CPL/CPA benchmarks, and allocation opportunities.

## Input
- Channel performance data (spend, clicks, conversions by channel)
- Industry benchmark data

## Output
- Channel performance rankings
- Budget reallocation recommendations
- Emerging channel opportunities
