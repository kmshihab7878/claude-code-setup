# Landing Page Analyzer

Analyzes competitor landing pages for positioning, messaging, social proof, and conversion patterns. Extracts headlines, CTAs, pricing structures, and trust signals.

## Input
- Competitor URLs from ad-research intel dump

## Output
- Positioning analysis per competitor (headline, value prop, CTA, social proof)
- Conversion pattern identification (what they're optimizing for)
