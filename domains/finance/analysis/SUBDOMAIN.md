# Finance — Analysis

Price forecasting, anomaly detection, market scanning.

## Skills

- `finance-ml` — Price prediction models, portfolio optimization, risk metrics, technical indicators
- `timesfm-forecasting` — Zero-shot TimesFM forecasting (works on any univariate series; demo set includes global-temperature and covariates examples)
- `data-analysis` — Natural language → SQL/pandas (cross-domain; heavily used for market-data exploration)
- `anomaly-detector` — Metric-drift detection, adaptable to price/volume anomalies

## Agents

- **L2:** `data-analyst`
- **L3:** `finance-tracker`, `analytics-reporter`

## Notes

- `timesfm-forecasting` is **domain-agnostic** — the global-temperature demo proves it. Extraction candidate as a standalone forecasting product.
- `finance-ml` bundles common technical indicators and risk metrics; works without any aspirational MCP.
