## The end-to-end scenario

The original doc's strongest section was a single worked example of building, deploying, and trading a crypto trading bot that touched every domain. That walkthrough moved to [`docs/DEMO.md`](./DEMO.md) (scripted reproducible sequence) and [`skills/aster-trading/SKILL.md`](../skills/aster-trading/SKILL.md) (the domain-specific skill).

## Why this was consolidated

Hand-maintained capability counts drifted across 10+ files despite prior council remediation. The fix is structural: have one generated source of truth (`INVENTORY.md` via `scripts/inventory.sh`), validate drift in CI (`scripts/validate.sh`), and have every other doc link to it rather than restate counts. See `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md` §1 for the reasoning.
